Vaccum tube era

Things i learned about the Vacuum tube era, from the early 1900s to the 1950s, was a crucial time in electronics. Vacuum tubes, which amplify electrical signals, were used in radios, televisions, and early computers like ENIAC. They helped improve sound quality in radios and made the first TV broadcasts possible.

They enabled long-distance communication and helped create the first electronic circuits. However, Vacuum tube were bulky and generated a lot of heat,

leading to the invention of transistors in the late1950s, which replaced them in most devices. Despite being outdated, Vacuum tubes, played a crucial role in the development of modern electronics.

Introduction to Computer System

A computer system is a combination of hardware and software designed to receive, process, store, and output data. It encompasses several components that work together to perform a variety of tasks.

There are 4 Computer components Hardware, Software, People ware, and Data ware.

A computer system is an integration of hardware, software, peopleware, and dataware. Hardware encompasses the physical components like the CPU, RAM, and storage devices that execute software instructions.

Software includes the operating systems and applications that tell the hardware what to do. Peopleware refers to the individuals who interact with, manage, and use these systems. Dataware represents the information processed and managed by the system. Together, these elements enable computer systems to perform a wide range of tasks efficiently.
